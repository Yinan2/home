About

Doctoral researcher in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at McGill University. My current work studies neural radiance and gaze fields for modeling human attention in 3D environments and relighting with diffusion-assisted radiance fields. Previously, I developed age-specific image quality assessment metrics and tools.

Research

NeRF Gaze & Attention Relighting IQA
Foundation Model for 3D Attention & Relighting
McGill • 2023—present

Training NeRFs for 3D scene representation; collecting shoppers’ eye-tracking data; modeling human attention with neural radiance and gaze fields; merging NeRFs for faster training; combining diffusion with NeRF for relighting.

Image Quality Assessment Metrics
Google DeepMind • 2023—2024

Personalized image quality metric combining benefits of multiple feature spaces. Integrated DINOv2 and I-JEPA embeddings (Fréchet/LPIPS), added MMD for distribution distance, evaluated robustness across semantic subspaces and transformations.

Age-Specific IQA
McGill • 2020—2022

Designed age-specific perceptual IQA; image enhancement and simulation for different age groups; implemented and evaluated IQA algorithms; psychophysical experiments to validate the metric.

Computational Photography Portfolio
McGill • 2021

Fusion, super-resolution, dehazing, colorization, matting & compositing, inpainting, and neural style transfer.

Selected Engineering & Earlier Work

Image Adaptive Filtering

Traditional denoising; adaptive Gaussian, Wiener, and bilateral filters; MSE/PSNR evaluation.

Log-Gabor Image Processing

Image reconstruction with log-Gabor wavelets and FFT; magnitude/phase roles; MATLAB GUI for symmetric patterns.

User-Centric Federated Learning

Python algorithms for user-centric FL with improved distributed model accuracy.
